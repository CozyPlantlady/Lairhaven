FAQs:

Most asked questions. Check these before contacting customer care!

Where is the *** location?

Unfortunately we can't tell the exact location until the contract is written.

What do I pay?

You pay monthly fee for the location, unless you want to buy it all for your self! Please contact customer care for any given time to change your contract. There is additional fee if you want to get your place decorated by us, which we do gladly! You can also choose to use our ninjas for when you are moving in sneakily. Ninjas are for moving only, and can't be hired as henchmen.

I'm not a designer, how to I make my hideout look nice?

Are you an up and coming hero, but still looking for your personal style? Is your parents basement starting to feel too cramped for all the gadgets? Are you the worst nightmare our city has ever seen, but your crib looks like granma's tearoom? Not to worry, we are here for you! We do personalized hideout decorations!

What happens if I *krhm* get caught?

Your contract is binding 2 months after you cancel it + any additional fees. We will however empty the location for you.

Who owns the company?

The founder of the company wants to remain anonymous, but used to go around as "The Prettifier". The Prettifier has a long history of decorating houses. At first they used to do it without permission.
"I was quite a rascal in my youth, and was often called chaotic neutral. I used to break in to any house and re-decorate it. It didn't matter who was living there. One time I broke in to the Batcave and made it look like Hello Kitty was living there!"
"It was a lucky coincidence that was able to make a career out of it. Someone had heard about me and asked if I wanted to re-decorate their lair! First I was against it, but then I was offered a lot of money and said yes!"
